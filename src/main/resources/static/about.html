About AmazeCare Hospital

AmazeCare Hospital focuses on delivering quality healthcare by ensuring smooth patient registration, easy appointment scheduling, proper medical record maintenance, and effective doctor consultations.

Patient Care
Register, maintain personal details, find doctors, manage appointments and view completed consultations.
Doctor Workflow
View upcoming appointments, conduct consultations, recommend tests, create records and issue prescriptions.
Administrative Control
Manage doctors, patients, appointment rescheduling and high-level reporting from one dashboard.
